About Josh

A CPA who's sat on
both sides of the table.

Joshua Jolley is a skilled finance advisor and former CFO with extensive experience in accounting, budgeting, and management. He has a solid educational background, receiving a Bachelor of Business Administration in Accounting from Lee University. Before his current position at Titan Autonomous Corp, he served as CPA Manager at the highly regarded firm, Henderson Hutcherson & McCullough. Joshua also held various management roles in different organizations, including Chick-fil-A Corporate. At Lee University, he managed accounting and budget coordination, giving him an appreciation for academia's financial aspects.

Joshua's expertise lies in finance, accounting, budgeting, management, and leadership. He is an accomplished professional with years of experience and a passion for helping organizations thrive financially.

Our Services

Support at every stage of your finances

From individual tax returns to full CFO-level oversight, here's how we can help.

Tax Preparation & Planning

Accurate, timely tax filing for individuals and businesses, paired with year-round planning strategies designed to minimize liability and avoid surprises at filing time.

Bookkeeping

Reliable, up-to-date books that give you a clear picture of where your business stands — including reconciliations, financial statements, and ongoing transaction management.

Business Consulting

Practical guidance on entity structure, cash flow, growth planning, and operational efficiency — helping business owners make confident, informed financial decisions.

Year-Round CPA Support

Advisory & Compliance

Proactive alerts on regulatory changes, filing deadlines, and compliance requirements — so nothing catches you off guard and every deadline is met.
